Personal Reflection

Why I Am Ready to Grow in Cybersecurity

This reflection answers the required company-based questions and connects my personal strengths, current skills, and career goals.

Dream Company

Bytes Security, Johor

I want to work with Bytes Security because the company offers exposure to SOC and penetration testing. I believe a smaller cybersecurity company can give me more hands-on experience, closer guidance, and real tasks that help me learn faster.

Value I Can Bring

I can bring strong willingness to learn, networking knowledge, Wireshark practice, Linux familiarity, and interest in phishing/email investigation. I am also able to support documentation, alert review, basic troubleshooting, and teamwork.

Why Hire Me?

I am suitable for an internship or entry-level security role because my academic background is directly related to Computer Network Security. I am honest about my current level, but I actively improve through certifications, labs, projects, and self-learning.

Suitable Skills

My suitable skills include networking fundamentals, Cisco Packet Tracer, Linux, Wireshark, Python, Java, basic penetration testing concepts, SQL injection basics, teamwork, time management, adaptability, and attention to detail.

Strengths

My strengths are learning adaptability, patience in technical practice, teamwork, analytical thinking, and interest in understanding how cyber threats happen. I am also motivated by clear goals and continuous improvement.

Weaknesses

My weakness is that I still need more real industry exposure with SIEM tools, incident handling, and advanced investigation. To improve this, I plan to practise Splunk or QRadar basics, strengthen log analysis, and learn SOC workflow through labs and internship experience.

Why a Company Would Choose Me

A company would choose me because I have a clear cybersecurity career direction, relevant academic background, real evidence of learning, and a realistic growth plan. I may still be at an early stage, but I am serious about becoming a capable SOC Analyst who can contribute to monitoring, investigation, documentation, and incident response support.
